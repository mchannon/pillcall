The Problem. Our Solution.

Existing smart pill dispensers cost $80–$300 and replace bottles entirely. PillCall fits onto what patients already own — no behavior change required.

⚠️ The Problem

  • 50% of patients skip or double-dose medications
  • 125,000 deaths/year from non-adherence in the US
  • $300B+ in avoidable hospitalizations annually
  • Competitors require replacing the bottle — massive friction
  • LifeAlert-style systems run $30–$50/month

✅ PillCall's Solution

  • Screws onto any existing prescription bottle
  • Texts you when you forget. Silent when you remember.
  • $10/month subscription — cancel anytime. No hidden fees.
  • Zero patient data collected — privacy by design
  • Four sizes cover the most common US bottle threads
