Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

1. Who We Are

PillCall Inc ("PillCall", "we", "us", or "our") operates pillcall.com and the PillCall smart pill cap service. Our contact information is (505) 666-5432 and plasticdotcheap@gmail.com.

2. The Short Version

PillCall is built on a foundational privacy principle: we never know what medication you take, and we never will. The cap detects open/close events. That's it. No drug names, no dosage, no diagnosis — ever.

3. Information We Collect

When you use PillCall, we collect only what is necessary to deliver the service:

  • Phone number(s) — to send SMS reminders and alerts
  • Cap open/close events and timestamps — to determine whether a dose was taken
  • WiFi network credentials — stored locally on the cap only, never transmitted to our servers
  • Name and email — if provided during preorder or account setup

We do not collect medication names, dosage information, diagnoses, health records, or any other medical information.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To send SMS dose reminders and double-dose alerts to your designated phone numbers
  • To fulfill preorders and process payments
  • To respond to customer service inquiries

5. We Do Not Sell Your Data. Ever.

PillCall will never sell, rent, license, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. This is not a policy subject to change — it is a founding principle of this company.

6. SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number and using the PillCall service, you consent to receive SMS messages from PillCall related to your medication reminders and account. Message frequency varies based on your configured schedule. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

7. Data Retention

We retain event logs (cap open/close timestamps) for 90 days to support the reminder service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Third-Party Services

PillCall uses Twilio to deliver SMS messages. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy. We do not use advertising networks, analytics trackers, or data brokers.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at (505) 666-5432 or plasticdotcheap@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We will not retroactively weaken privacy protections for data already collected.
